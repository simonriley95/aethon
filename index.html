Aethon Maritimes LLP

A Globally Connected Freight Forwarding & Logistics Company

Transforming international cargo movement with reliable, technology-enabled logistics solutions, Aethon Maritimes LLP delivers seamless freight forwarding services for businesses across global markets. Headquartered in Gujarat, we provide end-to-end logistics support across ocean freight, air freight, customs clearance, inland transportation, and supply chain coordination.

Backed by a growing global partner network, we manage shipments with precision, transparency, and efficiency—ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time. Whether it’s a full container load, urgent air cargo, industrial equipment, or commercial consignments, your shipment is handled with professionalism and care.
